# Learning Content Rules

## N5 Focus
- Hiragana and katakana mastery
- Core beginner vocabulary
- Basic kanji recognition and reading
- Essential particles and sentence patterns
- Very simple reading and listening practice

## Teaching Style
- Keep explanations short
- Prefer examples before theory
- Follow every concept with practice
- Recycle weak items often
- Use clear beginner-friendly English

## Content UX
- Break learning into short chunks
- Avoid overwhelming users with too many new items at once
- Show progress through micro-milestones
- Make mistakes feel recoverable and normal

## Avoid
- Textbook-style long explanations
- Advanced grammar in beginner flows
- Explanations that assume prior Japanese knowledge
- Large unbroken content blocks without interaction
